A batch of fixes and small improvements:

- Scrolling up in a chat thread and stopping no longer makes the conversation jump or shake.
- In-review workspaces no longer sometimes open to an empty "No session selected" panel instead of their conversation.
- Archiving a workspace now stops that workspace's running scripts and terminals instead of leaving them as orphan processes.
- Sidebar drag-and-drop no longer gets stuck in a phantom drag after a trackpad tap.
- Apple-keyboard media keys (play/pause, next, previous, fast-forward, rewind) now reach Spotify, Apple Music, and the system Now Playing app instead of triggering a "pop" inside the app.
- The "Inject sessions" scrollbar is hidden while the session chips stay horizontally scrollable.
- A language picker is now available during onboarding, not just in Settings.
